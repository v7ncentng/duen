Our Purpose

Mission Statement

DUEN fosters interdisciplinary collaboration within the engineering space — connecting like-minded engineers with the opportunities they need to advance their careers and leave a lasting mark.

Our Core Values

01 / 03

Camaraderie

By bringing together engineers from every discipline, DUEN members forge tight-knit bonds and find a second family at Davis — from bingsoo runs to ski trips in Tahoe.

02 / 03

Professionalism

Through workshops, an alumni network, and an officer internship program, DUEN members gain the professional edge that industry demands.

03 / 03

Community Service

Future engineers create impact through innovation — and also through action. DUEN invests in the greater Davis community, broadening our perspective on how engineering serves others.

03 —

Alumni Network

DUEN graduates go on to shape the industry. As an officer, you unlock full access to our alumni network — bridging Davis to some of the world's most sought-after companies.
